Don't Get Caught by The Phaser Dude!

  • Originality: Design for fast mouse clicking skill. I don't think there's a similar game yet.
  • Technical Merit: Uses the Arcade Physics engine to create sprites which can bounce off of each other, bounce off the wall, and have gravity.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I still cannot change the music between game play and victory/game over. The game music just stops and the victory or game over music won't play.
  • High Concept: Fast reflex clicking the mouse.
  • Theme: Theme: Caught.
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Mouse. Randomness: None. Play style: Single player, arcade.
  • Player Experience Goals: Have fun.
  • Gameplay: Click on the blue ball to bounce it. Physics applied here. Clicking on the bottom of the ball will make it fly up. Clicking on the top of the ball will make it fall down faster. Clicking on the left of the ball will make it fly to the right. Clicking on the right of the ball will make it fly to the left. Do not let the Phaser dude catch it, if he does, you lose. If he cannot catch you in 30 seconds, you win. If you are fast enough, you can keep the ball from ever falling down to the ground. The Phaser dude's speed will progressively increase as the game progress.
  • Target Audience: Everyone.
  • Play Time: Maximum 30 seconds.
  • Strategies: Try to click fast to keep the ball from falling down.
