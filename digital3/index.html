Click! Click!

  • High Concept: Testing mouse click input.
  • Theme: Theme: Testing.
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Mouse.
  • Player Experience Goals: Test out mouse input.
  • Gameplay: Click the red hexagon to have it start moving. Try to click in the middle of if to stop it. If click is not exactly in the middle, it will change direction.
  • Target Audience: Everyone.
  • Play Time: Minimum 1 second.
  • Strategies: Try to click accurately.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Borrow code here: https://phaser.io/examples/v2/arcade-physics/move-to-pointer, but added click to stop feature.
