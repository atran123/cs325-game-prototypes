Shoot the Chickens!

  • High Concept: "Duck Hunt" style game. Shoot the flying chickens with mouse click or touch.
  • Theme: Chicken.
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Mouse, or touch. Randomness: chickens (sprites) position, speed. Play style: Single player, arcade.
  • Player Experience Goals: Have fun?
  • Gameplay: As the chickens are flying across the screen, the player will attempt to shoot them down by clicking on them or touching on them.
  • Target Audience: All.
  • Play Time: 1-2 minutes.
  • Strategies: Fast response and judgement.
