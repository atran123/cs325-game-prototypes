Shoot the Choppers!

  • High Concept: Helicopters shooting game. Shoot the flying choppers.
  • Theme: Arcade.
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Keyboard. Randomness: choppers (sprites) position, speed. Play style: Single player, arcade.
  • Player Experience Goals: Have fun?
  • Gameplay: As the choppers are flying across the screen, the player will attempt to shoot them down. Every time the player can fire a single bullet, and cannot fire again until the bullet hit a chopper, or goes out of bound.
  • Target Audience: Young kids.
  • Play Time: 1-2 minutes.
  • Strategies: Fast response and judgement.
  • Prototype Assets: Borrowed from the internet. Sources: google.

    • Music: Space Invaders soundtrack Super Mario Bros
