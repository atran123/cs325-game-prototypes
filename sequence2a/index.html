Dodge the Dog

  • High Concept: 2-player chess type game. Chess type game.
  • Theme: Chess.
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Mouse. Play style: Single player vs. Computer.
  • Player Experience Goals: Have fun. The goal of the game is to avoid moving across the dog, or running out of time, and to press your opponent into one of those 2 conditions
  • Gameplay: Each side will have 2 pieces, on a simple board. Computer's pieces are purple. Player's pieces are green. Player's pieces are at bottom. Player's left piece goes first. One piece can be moved per turn. Chess pieces can be moved horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to an empty position. Player start with a time limit of 8 seconds to move. Each successful move earns a point. As the score goes up, the time limit will gradually goes down to 1 second. The player or computer loses when he/she has no choice but to move a piece across the dog, or time is up.
  • Target Audience: All.
  • Play Time: 3-5 minute.
  • Strategies: Try to press both pieces of your opponent into 1 side of the board, and try to avoid being pressed to 1 side.
  • Originality: The game strategy. Can move a piece by just clicking on it, no need to slide it. Decreasing time with increasing score.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype: developing the game with states, chess algorithm. What was the easiest or hardest part of making it. Hard part: graphics and computer algorithm.
